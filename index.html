Program Overview

A contemporary BBA designed for the digital economy, delivered from an engineering-powered campus.

Curriculum & Structure

Six semesters covering core management, quantitative techniques, business analytics, and technology-enabled business. Electives in digital marketing, fintech, HR analytics, and operations strategy.

AJCE Advantage

Shared ecosystem with engineering, AI, and innovation departments. Exposure to hackathons, startup cells, and interdisciplinary projects that blend management and technology.

Career Outcomes

Roles in marketing, banking, consulting, HR, and operations. Strong pathway to MBA and other postgraduate programs with support for competitive exam preparation.

Admission & Eligibility

Clear, transparent criteria to help you plan your application.

Who Can Apply

Any +2 (Class XII) pass candidate from Science, Commerce, or Humanities with minimum 50% aggregate marks from a recognized board. Relaxation is provided as per government norms for eligible categories.

Selection Process

Shortlisting based on +2 marks and application details, followed by personal interaction or counseling. Management quota seats are available under institutional guidelines.

Fee & Scholarships

Fee structured to remain affordable while providing premium facilities. Merit scholarships, need-based support, and special schemes may be available for deserving students.

Why Choose AJCE BBA

A management degree anchored in one of Kerala’s most respected engineering institutions.

Faculty & Mentoring

Experienced faculty with academic and corporate backgrounds guide you through concepts and real-world applications. Regular mentoring helps you build confidence and clarity in your career path.

Industry Interface

Guest talks, workshops, and live projects with companies in IT, manufacturing, services, and startups. Internship support that helps you experience how businesses work on the ground.

Campus Life & Facilities

Digitally enabled classrooms, rich library resources, sports, clubs, and student associations. A disciplined yet vibrant campus culture that focuses on holistic development.

FAQs

Is this a regular on-campus program?
Yes.The BBA program is conducted in regular mode with on-campus classes, attendance requirements, internal assessments, and examinations as per academic regulations.
Can non-commerce students apply?
Yes. Students from Science and Humanities streams can also apply, provided they meet the minimum eligibility marks in +2.
Is there support for higher studies?
Yes,The department provides guidance for MBA and other postgraduate options, along with inputs for aptitude tests, group discussions, and interviews.
